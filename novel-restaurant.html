Our Story

A culinary treasure in the heart of Kansas City

Tucked inside the vibrant East Crossroads Arts District, Novel offers a dining experience built on intention. Our dining room centers on a fifty-foot hand-laid tile mosaic — a work of art that sets the stage for the art on every plate.

An open kitchen, a full-service granite bar seating eighteen, and a dog-friendly patio planted with native grasses make Novel as welcoming as it is remarkable. Every detail is chosen to create space for unhurried conversation and genuine hospitality.
